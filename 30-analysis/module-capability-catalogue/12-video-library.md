# 12 — Video Library

## Purpose
A curated library of educational video lessons for every subject and level — free, on-demand learning
for students and lifelong learners.

---

## Full Capabilities
- **Catalogue** — organised by subject (Mathematics, Science, AI & ML, Coding & Dev, History, Language
  Learning, exam-prep, skills, and more); by level and language; playlists and multi-video courses.
- **Discover** — search, sort, and filter; recommendations; trending and new; teacher/channel pages.
- **Player** — in-app playback, speed control, captions/subtitles, quality settings, resume position,
  playback queue, and picture-in-picture.
- **Learning Tools** — notes at timestamps, bookmarks, attached materials, linked quizzes, transcripts,
  AI summary of a video, and translated captions.
- **Personalisation** — watch history, watch-later, continue-watching, and follow subjects/teachers.
- **Offline & Accessibility** — download for offline (rights permitting); low-bandwidth mode; captions;
  screen-reader; multi-language.
- **Creation & Curation** — teacher uploads with review/moderation; Corporation-curated playlists.
- **Content Operations (behind the scenes)** — sourcing (public/educational and partners), rights, and
  moderation.

---

## Content / Data It Draws On
- Video catalogue and metadata (subject, level, language, teacher) and video streams.
- Per-resident watch history and notes; transcripts and AI summaries.

## Who Uses It
- **Students, aspirants, and lifelong learners**; **teachers** as creators (future).

---

## Notes for the Prioritisation Pass (later — not decisions)
- The demo has curated videos (via embeds), categories, search/sort, and playback.
- Playlists, learning tools, offline, teacher uploads, and self-hosting/rights are the main build-out;
  note the current third-party (YouTube) streaming dependency.
