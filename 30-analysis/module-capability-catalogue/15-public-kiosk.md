# 15 — Public Kiosk (Self-Service)

## Purpose
A touch-screen public access point so residents without a personal device or internet can use the
full platform — in libraries, ward offices, community centres, and public spaces.

---

## Full Capabilities
- **Self-service interface** — a portrait-mode touch UI with large tiles (Browse Books, My Account,
  Borrow/Return Book, Test Prep, Audio & Podcasts, Help Desk); on-screen date/time; Corporation
  branding; and an idle/attract screen.
- **Voice search & assistance** — spoken search and on-screen prompts for low-literacy and hands-free
  use.
- **Library card & identity** — tap or scan the digital library card (or Aadhaar/DigiLocker) to sign
  in, with automatic session logout for privacy on a shared device.
- **Borrow & return at the kiosk** — reserve, borrow, and return physical books; print or scan
  receipts — a physical touchpoint for the phygital lending flow.
- **On-screen access to platform content** — read eBooks, listen to audiobooks and podcasts, browse
  newspapers, and take test-prep, directly at the kiosk.
- **Accessibility** — large touch targets, audio guidance, screen-reader support, wheelchair-friendly
  placement, and multi-language, for elderly and differently-abled residents.
- **Help & support** — a Help Desk tile, on-screen guidance, and assisted flows.
- **Deployment & fleet management** — kiosks across libraries, ward offices, community centres, and
  public spaces, with remote monitoring, content sync, and central admin management of the kiosk fleet.

---

## Content / Data It Draws On
- All platform content and per-resident data (via library-card / identity sign-in).
- Physical-stock and lending data (for borrow/return).
- Kiosk fleet, configuration, and health/monitoring data.

## Who Uses It
- **Residents without a personal device or internet**, walk-in library visitors, and **senior
  citizens**.
- **Corporation staff** — kiosk fleet management via the Admin module.

---

## Notes for the Prioritisation Pass (later — not decisions)
- Reuses the web platform, phygital lending, and identity foundations rather than rebuilding them.
- Requires physical hardware procurement, installation, and network at each site — a deployment
  dependency to weigh against the timeline.
