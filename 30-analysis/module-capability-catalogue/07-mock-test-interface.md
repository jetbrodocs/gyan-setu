# 07 — Mock Test Interface

## Purpose
A realistic, timed exam-hall experience so every aspirant can practise under real conditions and learn
precisely where they stand.

---

## Full Capabilities
- **Exam Modes** — full-length timed mock tests; sectional tests; topic quizzes; previous-year papers;
  custom practice mode; adaptive tests.
- **Test Experience** — full-screen mode; live countdown timer; question navigator (answered / marked
  / skipped at a glance); Save & Next, Mark for Review, Clear Response; exam-accurate question types;
  negative marking per exam pattern; multi-language question display.
- **Integrity** — instructions/consent screen; auto-submit on timeout; optional proctoring-lite for
  high-stakes tests.
- **Results & Analysis** — instant scored result (overall %, correct/incorrect/unanswered,
  subject-wise breakdown, time-per-question); comparison to peers / percentile / rank; full solution
  review with explanations; bookmark tough questions.
- **Learning Loop** — retake, targeted practice on weak areas, add wrong questions to revision; every
  attempt saved permanently to the Personal Dashboard.
- **Accessibility** — extra-time settings, large text, and screen-reader support for eligible
  residents.

---

## Content / Data It Draws On
- Test definitions, question sets, and marking schemes.
- Per-resident attempts, answers, scores, and timings; comparative/percentile data.

## Who Uses It
- **Competitive-exam aspirants** and **students**.

---

## Notes for the Prioritisation Pass (later — not decisions)
- The demo has a full timed flow, question navigator, negative marking, detailed results, and
  persistence.
- Sectional/adaptive/previous-year modes, deeper solution review, percentile/comparison, proctoring,
  and accessibility settings are the main build-out.
