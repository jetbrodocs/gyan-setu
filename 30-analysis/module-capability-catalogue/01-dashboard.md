# 01 — Dashboard (Home & Discovery)

## Purpose
Every resident's personalised starting point — one screen that welcomes them, shows what matters to
them, and takes them anywhere on the platform in a single tap.

---

## Full Capabilities
- **Personalised Home** — welcome by name; "continue reading / listening / resume test" panels; quick
  stat tiles (books read, hours, tests, certificates, streak); a snapshot of the resident's day.
- **Discovery & Trending** — trending in your city/ward; new arrivals across all content types;
  "for you" recommendations spanning books, audiobooks, tests, videos, podcasts; featured and
  editorial collections; spotlight on local and heritage content.
- **Unified Search** — a single search bar across every content type (books, audiobooks, newspapers,
  tests, videos, podcasts, manuscripts) with suggestions, filters, and language-first results.
- **Navigation** — collapsible sidebar to every module; quick links, shortcuts, and recently visited.
- **Notifications & Reminders** — a notification centre for new content, activity reminders (continue
  reading, test due, book due), achievements, and announcements.
- **Announcements & Civic Messaging** — Corporation banners and notices, events, workshops, scheme
  awareness, and public campaigns surfaced to residents.
- **Personal shortcuts** — pinned/favourite modules, saved items, and goals at a glance.
- **Language & Accessibility** — language switcher; accessibility quick settings (text size, contrast);
  voice search.

---

## Content / Data It Draws On
- Aggregated per-resident activity and progress across all modules.
- Trending/popularity signals; catalogue metadata across every content type.
- Corporation announcements and notifications.

## Who Uses It
- **Every resident**, on login — the universal entry point.
- **The Corporation** — to surface announcements and featured content (via the Admin module).

---

## Notes for the Prioritisation Pass (later — not decisions)
- Welcome, continue-reading, stat tiles, trending, and sidebar navigation largely exist in the demo.
- Unified cross-content search, the notification centre, civic messaging, and voice search are the
  main net-new build.
