# 02 — Digital Library

## Purpose
The front door to the Corporation's entire collection of written knowledge: a place where any resident
can find, understand, and start reading any book — across every language and subject — in seconds, for
free.

---

## Full Capabilities
*The complete breadth of what this module can do, at a glance. Nothing here is filtered for effort or
phase; each line can be detailed further at finalisation.*

- **Browse & Discover** — visual catalogue of covers; curated collections (classics, regional Gujarati,
  academic, science, business, children's, local-author / Corporation-exclusive); themed and seasonal
  reading lists; dynamic shelves (new arrivals, trending, most-read, staff picks, award winners);
  personalised recommendations; author and series pages.
- **Search & Filter** — search by title, author, and narrator, plus full-text search inside books;
  filter by language, format, subject, collection, reading level, and availability; sort by relevance,
  rating, popularity, and date; spelling tolerance and results in the resident's language first.
- **Book Detail & AI Understanding** — a rich detail page per title; AI-generated summaries at multiple
  depths; visual mind-map; auto-generated quiz; spoken (audio) summary; all available and translatable
  into the resident's language; confidence/trust signals; links to the audiobook or video version.
- **Read & Access** — one-tap "Read Now" into the eBook Reader; offline download (subject to content
  rights); cross-format continuity (switch between reading and listening); large-print and PDF formats.
- **Physical copy access (phygital)** — where the Corporation holds physical stock: check availability,
  reserve or borrow a physical book, choose branch pickup or home delivery, and manage returns,
  renewals, due dates, and any fines — a digital-to-doorstep bridge between the online catalogue and
  the Corporation's physical library branches.
- **Personalisation & Reader Tools** — My Bookshelf and personal lists; reading history; continue-
  reading; requests for titles the library doesn't yet hold; personal reading goals and challenges.
- **Community & Social** — resident ratings and reviews (moderated); popularity signals; recommend and
  share titles; book clubs and reading circles; report errors or content.
- **Accessibility & Language Inclusion** — full four-language parity as the goal; large-print,
  high-contrast, dyslexia-friendly, screen-reader and keyboard support; on-demand translation; a safe,
  filtered children's / young-readers area.
- **Curation & Content Operations (behind the scenes)** — librarian tools to add, edit, feature, and
  collection titles; metadata and cover enrichment; digital rights and licensing controls (concurrent
  access, borrowing windows, downloads); integration with the physical library catalogue; moderation.

---

## Content / Data It Draws On
- The book catalogue (covers, titles, authors, publishers, years, languages, formats, subjects,
  collections, ratings) and the full text/PDF of each title.
- AI-generated summaries, mind-maps, quizzes, and audio summaries per title.
- Licensing / rights metadata, and (for phygital) physical-stock and branch data.
- Per-resident data: bookshelf, reading history, ratings/reviews, requests, recommendations.
- Links to matching audiobook and video versions.

## Who Uses It
- **Every resident** (students, exam aspirants, seniors, general readers) — the primary users, with
  tailored discovery.
- **Children / young readers** — via the safe, filtered area.
- **Librarians / Corporation administrators** — curation, collections, and rights (via the Admin module).

---

## Notes for the Prioritisation Pass (later — not decisions)
- Browse, search, book detail + AI, and read/access largely exist in the demo; community, full
  four-language parity, rights controls, full-text search, and phygital lending are the largest
  net-new build.
- Phygital lending (reservations, pickup, delivery, returns, fines) ties into borrowing identity /
  eligibility and the Admin module (physical stock) — de-duplicate ownership during the prioritisation
  pass.
