# 08 — Podcast Creation Studio

## Purpose
Lets local teachers, experts, and residents create and publish audio knowledge — turning the platform
into a community-owned voice, not just a consumption service.

---

## Full Capabilities
- **Recording Studio** — browser-based recording with live waveform, background noise cancellation,
  multi-take, and pause/resume; upload existing audio.
- **Editing** — trim, splice, and re-record segments; add intro/outro and background music; AI
  auto-transcription; auto-generated show notes and captions.
- **Series & Episodes** — organise into named series with language tags, cover art, descriptions, and
  categories; schedule and publish; seasons.
- **Publishing & Distribution** — publish to the platform's Podcasts Library; optional external
  distribution / RSS.
- **Creator Analytics** — listeners, plays, completion, ratings, and subscriber counts, per episode
  and series, with trends.
- **Recognition** — creator badges, "featured creator," and future recognition/tips mechanics.
- **Governance** — creator onboarding/approval; content review and moderation workflow; guidelines;
  rights and consent.
- **Listen side (Podcasts Library)** — residents browse by series/topic/language, play with a built-in
  player, subscribe/follow, download offline, rate, share, and resume.

---

## Content / Data It Draws On
- Audio recordings, transcripts, and series/episode metadata and cover art.
- Creator profiles; listener analytics; moderation status.

## Who Uses It
- **Approved content creators** (teachers, experts, residents) on the create side.
- **Every resident** on the listen side; **administrators** for moderation.

---

## Notes for the Prioritisation Pass (later — not decisions)
- The demo has recording, publishing, consumer listening, and creator stats.
- Editing tools, transcription accuracy, distribution, subscriptions, offline listening, and the
  moderation workflow are the main build-out.
