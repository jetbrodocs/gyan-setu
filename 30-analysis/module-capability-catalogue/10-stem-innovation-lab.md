# 10 — STEM Innovation Lab

## Purpose
Gives every student hands-on modern skills — coding, robotics, AI, electronics — regardless of their
school's resources, aligned to national education (NEP) and Atal innovation goals.

---

## Full Capabilities
- **Course Catalogue** — categories (3D Design, AI & ML, Atal Innovation, Coding & Dev, Electronics &
  IoT, Robotics Kits); structured courses → modules → lessons; beginner-to-advanced levels;
  age/grade tracks.
- **Learn** — video lessons paired with an in-browser code editor (write, run, see output) across
  languages (Python, block/Scratch, JavaScript, and more); interactive tutorials; downloadable
  resources.
- **Practice & Build** — guided projects, circuit/robotics simulations, quizzes, and auto-graded
  assignments; a personal project portfolio.
- **Recognition** — XP, achievement badges, certificates, and a class leaderboard.
- **Live & Social** — live instructor workshops with booking; cohorts/classrooms; mentor Q&A; peer
  showcase; hackathons and competitions.
- **Teacher / School Tools** — assign to students, track class progress, integrate with Atal
  Tinkering Labs, and map to curriculum.
- **Guidance** — career and skill pathways; links to internships and opportunities.
- **Accessibility & Language** — multi-language lessons and a low-bandwidth mode.

---

## Content / Data It Draws On
- Course/lesson content, videos, and code exercises.
- Per-resident progress, XP, badges, certificates, and projects; workshop schedules; class/cohort data.

## Who Uses It
- **School and college students**, **teachers**, and **young innovators**.

---

## Notes for the Prioritisation Pass (later — not decisions)
- The demo has categories, video + code editor, badges, a leaderboard, and a live-workshop placeholder.
- A real code-execution sandbox, projects/grading, live-workshop booking, teacher tools, certificates,
  and simulations are the main build-out.
