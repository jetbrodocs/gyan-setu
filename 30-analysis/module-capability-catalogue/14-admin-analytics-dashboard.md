# 14 — Admin & Analytics

## Purpose
The Corporation's control room — to manage the platform's content, people, and access, and to
understand how residents use it, ward by ward, to guide investment and policy.

---

## Full Capabilities
- **Content Management** — add, edit, and curate every content type (books, audiobooks, newspapers,
  videos, tests, manuscripts, podcasts); build collections, featured shelves, and schedules; bulk
  import; metadata and cover management; rights/licensing; physical-stock/inventory for lending.
- **User & Role Management** — manage residents, students, seniors, creators, admins, and government
  officials; roles and permissions (role-based access); institutional/ward accounts; support tools.
- **Creator & Moderation** — approve creators; review and moderate community content (podcasts,
  reviews, uploads); handle reports; enforce guidelines.
- **Analytics & Insight** — KPIs (active users, content accessed, registrations, session time);
  DAU/MAU trends; content distribution; usage by time; top content; **ward-wise / geographic reach**;
  exam-prep engagement; learning outcomes; retention and funnels; custom reports; **exports
  (Excel/PDF/CSV)** and scheduled reports.
- **Civic & Governance Dashboards** — literacy and engagement indicators for policy; scheme-campaign
  reach; equity-of-access views (which wards/groups are underserved).
- **Announcements & Campaigns** — publish banners, notifications, civic messages, events, and featured
  content to residents.
- **Platform Config & Operations** — languages, feature flags, home/discovery curation; audit logs;
  data governance and privacy; backups; health and monitoring.
- **Integrations** — identity (Aadhaar/DigiLocker), physical-library systems, and other government
  systems (future).

---

## Content / Data It Draws On
- Everything: the full content catalogue, all user/role data, activity and analytics across every
  module, moderation queues, configuration, and audit logs.

## Who Uses It
- **Corporation administrators, librarians, content managers, and government officials**; support staff.

---

## Notes for the Prioritisation Pass (later — not decisions)
- Net-new: deferred/absent in the demo — this is the largest back-office build.
- Analytics depends on activity data being captured across every module; ward-wise reach depends on
  resident location/ward data being collected.
