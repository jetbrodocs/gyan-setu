# 09 — Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS Heritage)

## Purpose
Brings India's and the region's intellectual and cultural heritage to every resident — preserving
manuscripts, scriptures, and living traditions digitally for future generations.

---

## Full Capabilities
- **Historical Timeline** — browse six eras (Vedic → Mauryan → Gupta → Solanki → Mughal → Modern) with
  context; select an era to explore its works.
- **Manuscripts & Scriptures** — digitised manuscripts and texts with cover, title, language, era, and
  a summary of historical significance; filter by language and era.
- **Deep Reading** — a full manuscript reading view (parity with the eBook Reader), with translation,
  transliteration, a glossary of terms, and an AI companion to explain difficult classical texts.
- **Folk & Oral Traditions** — regional folk literature, oral storytelling, and songs (e.g. Saurashtra's
  folk literature) with audio, reusing the audiobook experience.
- **Immersive Heritage** — 360° virtual tours of monuments and sites (Modhera, Adalaj, and more);
  image galleries; audio guides; AR/VR experiences.
- **Local Heritage** — Corporation-specific and local history, monuments, personalities, and archives,
  with contributions from local historians.
- **Learning & Engagement** — curated heritage collections, articles, quizzes, a children's heritage
  section, and school tie-ins; events and exhibitions.
- **Language** — Sanskrit, Gujarati, Hindi, and English, with transliteration and translation.
- **Content Operations (behind the scenes)** — partnerships with academies and museums for
  digitisation; rights; curation.

---

## Content / Data It Draws On
- Manuscripts and texts with era/language metadata; audio for folk traditions; 360°/media assets;
  local-history archives; AI translations and explanations.

## Who Uses It
- **Every resident** — students, culture enthusiasts, seniors, and children; **local historians and
  institutions** as contributors.

---

## Notes for the Prioritisation Pass (later — not decisions)
- The demo has the era timeline and manuscript browsing.
- The full reading view, folk/oral traditions, 360° tours, translation/transliteration, local
  heritage, and digitisation partnerships are the main build-out. Digitisation is a partnerships
  workstream, not only engineering.
