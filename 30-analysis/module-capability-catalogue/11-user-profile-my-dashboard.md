# 11 — User Profile / My Dashboard

## Purpose
A personal knowledge home that remembers everything a resident does, recognises their progress, and
puts them in control of their experience and their data.

---

## Full Capabilities
- **Profile** — avatar, name, contact, reading streak, and join date; edit profile; privacy controls.
- **Activity & Stats** — reading/listening/learning activity charts (30-day and longer); totals
  (books, audiobooks, hours, tests, podcasts, courses); streaks and milestones.
- **My Content** — Currently Reading with progress bars; My Bookshelf/favourites; saved/clipped
  articles; playback queues; downloads for offline use.
- **Achievements** — earned certificates (downloadable and shareable), badges, completed courses, and
  a full test history with scores and dates.
- **Goals & Recommendations** — personal goals and challenges; personalised recommendations; reminders.
- **Settings** — language, dark mode, text size/accessibility, notification preferences, privacy and
  data controls, connected devices, and download management.
- **Account & Identity** — password and security; future Aadhaar/DigiLocker-linked account
  verification.
- **Data Ownership** — download my data; delete account; consent management.

---

## Content / Data It Draws On
- Aggregated per-resident activity across all modules; certificates; settings and preferences;
  account/identity.

## Who Uses It
- **Every resident**.

---

## Notes for the Prioritisation Pass (later — not decisions)
- The demo has the profile, stats, activity chart, currently-reading, certificates list, bookshelf,
  and test history, with visual-only settings.
- Functional settings, goals, data-ownership controls, identity linkage, and download management are
  the main build-out.
