# 06 — Test Preparation Hub

## Purpose
Coaching-grade exam preparation for every aspirant — free — across all major competitive exams, with
a clear, honest view of their readiness.

---

## Full Capabilities
- **Exam Catalogue** — major categories (UPSC, GPSC, SSC, Banking, GATE, JEE/NEET, Railways, Police,
  Teaching/TET, State PSCs, and more), each with sub-exams, syllabus, and available tests.
- **Practice** — daily practice question with explanation; topic-wise practice sets; previous-year
  questions; difficulty levels; flashcards.
- **Readiness Dashboard** — overall readiness %, subject-wise progress, average score, estimated
  rank/percentile, strengths and weaknesses, and trend over time.
- **Study Material** — syllabus breakdown; notes; linked books and videos; a current-affairs feed;
  exam notifications and calendar (dates, application windows).
- **Personalised Plan** — an AI study plan and schedule; recommended next topics; adaptive practice
  that targets weak areas.
- **Guidance & Doubt-solving** — exam strategy, tips, mentor/expert content; doubt-solving via AI and
  community.
- **Engagement** — streaks, badges, leaderboards, and study groups.
- **Links** — straight into the Mock Test Engine for full-length tests.

---

## Content / Data It Draws On
- Exam and question banks (MCQs, answers, explanations, tags), syllabi, exam calendars, current
  affairs.
- Per-resident performance and history; AI-generated study plans.

## Who Uses It
- **Competitive-exam aspirants** and **students**.

---

## Notes for the Prioritisation Pass (later — not decisions)
- The demo has exam categories, a daily question, and a readiness dashboard.
- Question-bank sourcing, study material, personalised/adaptive plans, exam notifications, and
  current affairs are the main build-out. Question-dataset sourcing is a content dependency.
