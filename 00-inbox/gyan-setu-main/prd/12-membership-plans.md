# Module 12: Membership Plans

**Reference Screenshot**: `slides/14-membership-plans.png`

## Description
Display-only page showing the three membership tiers and their features.

## Specifications

### Plan Cards (per screenshot)
- **BASIC** (₹0/month): Digital Catalog access, 5 eBook Downloads/month, Newspaper Archive (7 days)
- **STANDARD** (₹99/month): Unlimited Digital Reading, Full Audiobook Library, Full Newspaper Archive, 5 Mock Tests/month, Basic AI Summaries
- **GOLD** (₹199/month, "Most Popular" badge): All Standard + Unlimited Mock Tests & Analysis, Full AI Summaries & Chat, STEM Lab Premium Content, Offline Mode, Priority Support

### Additional Info
- 50% discount for Students & Senior Citizens (60+)
- Payment methods listed: UPI, Net Banking, Cards, BBPS
- DigiLocker & Aadhaar verification mentioned

### What's NOT Functional
- No payment gateway (no Razorpay/CCAvenue)
- No plan switching — user tier is pre-assigned in seed data
- "Choose Standard" / "Choose Gold" buttons are visual-only
- "Current Plan" indicator reads from seeded user data
