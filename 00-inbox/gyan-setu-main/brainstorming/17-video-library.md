# Module 17: Video Library — Brainstorming ✅ SCOPED

## Overview
Not in the original PRD/slides. Similar to Digital Library but for video content. Creators (mostly teachers) upload educational videos. Consumers browse, search, and watch.

## Questions & Decisions

### Q1: Content source
**Question:** Same approach as other modules — source a public video dataset or use YouTube embeds for seeded content?

**Answer:** YouTube embeds might work for now. Needs stakeholder confirmation — if they say no, fall back to public video dataset.

**Decision:** Default to YouTube embeds for seeded video content. Pending stakeholder approval — flagged for management discussion.

---

### Q2: Upload flow
**Question:** Should creators be able to upload videos, or just seeded content for browsing?

**Answer:** Seeded content only for now.

**Decision:** No upload flow for the demo. Consumer-side only — browse and watch seeded videos.

### Q3: Video player
**Question:** Custom player with features or simple embed?

**Answer:** Simple embed player is enough.

**Decision:** Standard/simple video player. No custom features like speed control or notes.
